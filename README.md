## Add, delete, and complete to-dos.

## To use:

    -   Add a to-do:
        -   Click in the 'Add New Todo' box.
        -   Type your to-do
        -   Hit enter on your keyboard
    -   Complete a to-do:
        -   Hover over your to-do
        -   Click to mark through
    -   Delete a to-do:
        -   Hover over your to-do
        -   Click the red trash can to delete
